Breakfast · 5 min · Serves 1

Microwave protein pancake bowl

High protein · Gluten free · Dairy free

Batter

  • Eggs 2
  • Banana ½
  • Protein powder 1 scoop
  • Cassava flour 1 tbsp

Toppings

  • Greek yogurt 2 tbsp
  • Papaya ½ cup
  • Blueberries ¼ cup
  • Maple syrup 1 tbsp

Method

  1. 1 Blend all batter ingredients until fluffy.
  2. 2 Pour into a microwave-safe bowl.
  3. 3 Microwave for 3 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. 4 Top with Greek yogurt, papaya, blueberries and a drizzle of maple syrup.

Macros are estimates. Protein powder brand may vary.
